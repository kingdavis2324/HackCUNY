About Us

Team Harambae is dedicated to unifying the body by streamlining fraternity and sorotity matchups. Our job is to help bright college students gain confidence in their candidacy as a member of Greek society and to gain more insight on the history and social environment of each Fraternity or Sorority.

Alpha Ϙ allows students to take an assessment that informally places students in a functioning CUNY chapter.
